## **04 main actions in the Trava Lending pool**

### **Deposit an asset in the lending pool.**

**Step 1.** Open [https://app.trava.finance/pool-list](https://app.trava.finance/pool-list)

**Step 2.** Connect your wallet and select the network that you want.

**Step 3.** Choose a pool:

<figure><img src=".gitbook/assets/act 6.png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**Step 4.** Open an asset detail by clicking dropdown arrow:

<figure><img src=".gitbook/assets/act 7.png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**Step 5.** Make a transaction:

* Enter the number of assets that you want to deposit in the box **“Amount”**
* Make sure you check all the current and updated information.
* Then click **“Deposit”.**

<figure><img src=".gitbook/assets/act 8.png" alt="" width="336"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**Step 6.** Waiting for the deposit processing: approve (if you deposit for the first time or approve amount larger than previous allowance amount) => executing transaction => Transaction succeeded.

**Notes**: Click **“See transaction detail”** to review your transaction.

### **Withdraw an asset**

**Step 1.** Open [https://app.trava.finance/pool-list](https://app.trava.finance/pool-list)

**Step 2.** Connect your wallet and select the network that you want.

**Step 3.** Choose a pool:

<figure><img src=".gitbook/assets/act 6.png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**Step 4.** Open an asset detail by clicking dropdown arrow:

<figure><img src=".gitbook/assets/act 7.png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**Step 5.** After that, your wallet notification will appear as below:

* Enter the number of assets that you want to withdraw in the box **“Amount”**
* Make sure you check all the current and updated information.

**Notes:** Click **“See transaction detail”** to review your transaction.
